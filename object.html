Specifications

Shape

I used basic 3D shapes and combined and cut-out parts of them to create new more obscure shapes that created these shapes to act as the toppings on the pizza object. For example I used a cylinder for the pepperoni, and I used half cylinders and a rectangle for the mushrooms.

Size

I used size in my model to distinguish the different elements of the object and give it a realistic look of a pizza. The contrast size of the pizza base framed inside the ring really makes the pizza toppings like the mushrooms and pepperoni pop and help with the aesthetic of my pizza object.

Colour

Colour was a big part of my model as it helped to finalise it all and to show and develop the different elements that I put on my object. The colours I used like the red on the pepperoni and the green on the capsicum give it a fun fresh look and gives it even more interest.
